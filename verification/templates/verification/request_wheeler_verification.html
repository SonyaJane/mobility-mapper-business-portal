What is Wheeler Verification?

Once three Wheelers have verified your business, you will earn a badge, as shown below.

Verified by Wheelers

This badge appears next to your listing in search results, and on both your public and private business profile pages. It helps customers identify trusted accessible venues at a glance.

How does it work?

You will be able to view all verification reports, and track your verification status at any time from your Business Dashboard.

While you are waiting for verification, wheelers browsing the Accessible Business Search will see the badge, shown below, next to your listing.

Verify the accessibility of this business and earn a £10 Amazon voucher

This lets the community know you're actively seeking verification. Wheelers can click the badge to view the verification request details and apply to verify your accessibility features. We only allow verified Wheelers to complete the verification process. We check each report for accuracy and completeness before approving the verification and making it available to you.
